Processing Steps

  1. Video → 2D Joint Detection (gewählter Detektor)
  2. 2D Keypoints → Body25 CSV (OpenPose-Format, 25 Joints)
  3. Body25 CSV → MocapNET v2.1 Neural Network → BVH (CMU Skeleton, 164 Joints)
